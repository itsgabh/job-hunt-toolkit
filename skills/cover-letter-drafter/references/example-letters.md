# Example Letters (your tone-calibration set)

This file holds a small set of cover letters **in your voice**, used by the
cover-letter-drafter for tone calibration — not as templates to copy. It ships
**empty**. As the skill drafts letters and you revise them until they sound like you,
paste the keepers here (one per opener archetype is ideal). The skill then reads them
to match your register on future drafts.

Keep every claim traceable to your `profile.md` — no inventions, no inflated metrics.

The four opener archetypes (see `cover-letter-patterns.md`):

1. **Direct-fit opener** — the role-fit alignment is unusually clean.
2. **Story opener** — a specific experience maps tightly to the role.
3. **Why-this-company opener** — you have genuine, substantiated resonance with the mission.
4. **Problem opener** — the JD names a clear problem you can speak to.

## What to check when you revise a draft into a keeper

1. Read it aloud. Flag any sentence that sounds auto-generated.
2. Check every claim against `profile.md` — traceable, no inflated metrics.
3. Word count lands at 350–450.
4. The close is direct, with a concrete next-step line.
5. You would actually open with that first line in a message to a recruiter you know.
